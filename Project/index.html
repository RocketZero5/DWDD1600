What we are all about

Our entire point is to allow an open and loosly regulated place to give people an open
place to discuss the Star Wars Franchise owned by LucusFilm.


This should be a place where you can...

  • Make friends
  • Have a free space to express yourself
  • Have Fun

We hope that you will be able to "heal" your interaction starved heart!

Rules

This may be an open place to just share the love but their are some Rules

  1. Be Respectful - Treat everyone as you would yourself
  2. Follow admin requests - IF they need you off, just listen. It's easier that way.
  3. Listen to the Policies - The Policies are like the banned list of things to do.

Policies

Essentially just don't be a jerk. However, to be Clear about it I will put a list below

We hope that as you enjoy your stay here you make new friends and find new perspectives on Star Wars!
